WHY WE?

Study Zone is a Coaching Centre has opened with the experience and success of our well defined and abled faculty. Over the past one year, our Centre in Jamshedpur has developed into the area’s primary resource for after-school tuition for children aged 4 to 22.

Our Beliefs

At our Centre our teachers believe that every child needs and deserves a good grasp of the basic skills of language and number. But we know that sometimes children lack the confidence they need to get the best out of school. Sometimes children need just that little bit of extra help. And sometimes the right help at a crucial moment can make all the difference. We believe that improving confidence, motivating through success and developing focus and concentration will bring the brightest possible future to your child. That’s where we come in.

Holiday Seminars

Study Zone’s Holiday Seminars are Year 11 and 12 revision courses held in in the Term 1, 2 and 3 school holidays at D.A.V Patel nagar School premises. Each seminar summarises important theory, key concepts and practical skills essential to a wide variety of Year PCM subjects. Each 3 hour session will focus on reviewing all key aspects of theory, followed by individual discussion and practical problem solving. You will also be provided with exclusive seminar notes, designed by Essentials authors.
